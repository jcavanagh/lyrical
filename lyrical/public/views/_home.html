Welcome to Lyrical!

Lyrical is an easy way to show exactly what a song means to you.

Try it!

Find A Song

Find a song and add its lyrics

You can include a link to SoundCloud or YouTube if you've got it!

Make It Yours

Take the lyrics and explain exactly what they mean to you

Highlight parts you like, parts you dislike, and why you feel that way.

A single phrase or an entire verse - it's up to you!

Make A Playlist

Organize songs however you like, with playlists

Whether by genre, by person or completely at random - there's no wrong way.

If you don't like playlists, no problem - they're not required.

Share

Share a song or a playlist with anyone

Facebook, Twitter, Google+, we've got it all!
